Volume II · After Team Select

More Marks · interface · as · icon ·

A second volume of proposed app icons — this set drawn directly from the visual grammar of TORCH's team select carousel: its helmet-stripe edge bands, its stadium-flood top wash, its tier badges, its position-dot indices, its gold-to-ember CTA gradient. Where Volume I treated icons as freestanding objects (a lapel pin, a struck coin, a heraldic crest), Volume II treats them as interface artifacts — each one a fragment of the game's own chrome, sampled at native scale and framed as a mark.
