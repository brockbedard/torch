HELMET VARIANTS

5 designs per team, 40 total. Each variant overrides shell color, facemask, stripe geometry, decal type, and optional finish (matte / chrome). Designed to ship to a future "alternate uniform" picker.
STRIPE TYPES — single (1 wide) · double (2 thin) · runway (3 stripes, accent flanking) · pinstripe (thick + 2 thin pinstripes) · outlined (stripe with contrast border) · none
DECAL TYPES — logo (team badge) · monogram (letter mark) · text (custom string) · blank (no decal)
FINISH — standard · matte (desaturated) · chrome (high-shine)
