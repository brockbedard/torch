Five home-screen archetypes for TORCH Football, each committing to a different strategic bet about what a player should feel in the first three seconds. Drawn from a research pass on Balatro, Slay the Spire, Inscryption, ESPN's 2024 College GameDay rebrand, and vintage gameday program covers. Tap any phone to open the full-size mockup at 390×844.

your team's helmet, waiting for you on the bench.

V
Plate 05 — Coin Toss Ritual

Coin Toss Ritual

a single verb. a single tap. a single coin.

The bet: extreme commitment. One struck-gold coin, slowly breathing under a narrow spotlight, arc-text around the rim, ember specks rising past it. Nothing else. No streak. No tiles. Everything in drawer.

Highest confidence Most shareable Hardest to nail Hides features

How to choose

Pick the bet you believe in.

These are five different arguments about what TORCH is. Stadium says it’s a night at the game. Program says it’s a ritual object from a more specific era. Locker says it’s your personal coaching career. Broadcast says it’s a sports network you launch. Coin says it’s a single sacred verb. You can’t split the difference — pick one, commit, and the rest of the app falls into line behind it.

Research is archived at docs/TORCH-HOMEPAGE-RESEARCH.md.

Recommendation
Strongest singular bets: II (Program) for TORCH-specific identity, or V (Coin) for maximum commitment.

Hybrid worth considering: Program chrome around a Coin central action — the masthead + date + matchup in printed-paper frame, but the primary action is tap-the-coin.

Skip for v1: Locker (needs personalization payoff); revisit for career mode.
