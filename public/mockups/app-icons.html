Concepts for the Home Screen Icon

Six
Marks · six · distinct · specimens ·

Six proposed app icons for TORCH Football, drawn exclusively from the two assets that define its on-screen identity: a four-layer flame and a nine-layer leather football. Each mark commits to a single idea — a lapel pin, a struck medallion, a broadcast bug, a heraldic crest, a motion poster, a chenille patch — and executes it with the craft the app hopes to earn.
