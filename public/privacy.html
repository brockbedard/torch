TORCH Football — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Data We Collect

TORCH Football does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data (scores, settings, progress) is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage.

Analytics

We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics services.

Advertising

TORCH Football contains no advertisements.

In-App Purchases

TORCH Football is free to play. Any future in-app purchases will be cosmetic only and clearly labeled.

Children's Privacy

TORCH Football is suitable for all ages (4+). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Third-Party Services

TORCH Football does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device. Clearing your browser data or app storage will delete all saved progress.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@torch.football
